directions:

Thanks for visiting the PregnancyTimer.com site! Our pregnancy calendar tool lets you choose the start date of your pregnancy using the larger calendar at the top right of the page.
Presto! Your can see your due date and all of the months of your pregnancy laid out in an easy to visualize way.
Our planning tool lets you see the growth of the baby month by month - information that you can find using the traditional pregnancy wheels...without twisting your head (or scratching your head?)
Easily see which week in your pregnancy you'll be in a given calendar month. Get a quick idea about "how pregnant" you'll be for social or work commitments tha will be easy to pick out on normal calendar months.
Click on dates on the lower row of months and the animated picture changes to show the common weight and length of a baby at that week in a pregnancy. The pregnancy "poster" also lists some basic things you'll experience during that "trimester" of your pregnancy.
Please use our pregnancy-timer tool as a starting place, not a substitute for consulting more detailed prenatal care sites or your doctor!
